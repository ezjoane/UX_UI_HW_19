MY UX SKILLS



USER RESEARCH

I usually love to use 1-1 interview, online survey, and proto persona to capture users’ pain, needs, and behaviors. Find out the potential user is an essential job in any of the UX design projects.

USER EMPATHY

Empathy is an ability that UX designers need to have to fully understand users’ needs, goals, and motivations. The tools that I used to do the user empathy are affinity diagram, empathy map, user persona, user journey map, etc.

QUALITATIVE INTERVIEWS

1-1 interview is an example of qualitative interviews. I can quickly get detailed feedback from the test user and ask some follow-up questions to them.


UX DESIGN PROJECTS

ONE BOUQUET APP

An app created for people who love flowers but don’t want to spend a lot of money on a beautiful custom arrangement. It fills the mid-level white space between grocery store flowers and flower delivery websites. Allows users the convenience & safety of ordering flowers from home.




ALEX'S LEMONADE STAND FOUNDATION REDESIGN

we have observed that the site Alex's Lemonade Stand is overwhelming with text, non-consistency of design, and hard to navigate. Our team aimed at helping improve the website's UI design and reorganize information to bring more attention to children's cancer, and we also want to make the user easy to donate and navigate through the site.




EASY TRIP APP

There're plenty of apps that people can choose to plan their travels at present. But within the increase in customers' needs, a single one existing platform can't satisfy them. EZ trip is made for people who have a tight budget for traveling and people who want to get some inspiration of where they want to visit.
